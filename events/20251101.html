NZKF national seminar in Wellington, November 2025

This seminar is for NZKF and IKYF registered practitioners only.

New Zealand Kyudo Federation will also be celebrating 20th anniversary of Wellington Kyudo Club, and honouring the memory of Adejola Musa Olatunji.

Wellington Kyudo Club is the first and oldest Kyudo Club in New Zealand, which was established with generous support from Sakai City in Japan.

Date:
Saturday 1st — Sunday 2nd November 2025

Instructors:

  • Kitahara, Osamu (北原 修) | Kyoshi 7 Dan | Matsumoto, Japan
  • Sato, Fumiko (佐藤 史子) | Kyoshi 6 Dan | Osaka, Japan
  • Yamashita, Hiromi (山下 博美) | Kyoshi 6 Dan | Osaka, Japan

Venue:
NZCT Stadium, Te Rauparaha Arena,
17 Parumoana Street, Porirua City, Wellington, New Zealand

Official transport:
We will arrange a bus to pick up participants from the official accommodation in Wellington CBD to the venue in Porirua City.
Please inform us during registration if you'd like to use the official transport.

Fees and payment

Do not pay before we've emailed you with the final fee.

Payments will be non-refundable, since we would have spent the money on venue and transport deposit.

Seminar fee:
Up to 300 NZD. To be confirmed after registration ends. The more people register, the lower the fee will be.

How to pay (NZKF members):
Please pay via your local club bank account and inform your club's committee.
Your club's secretary and treasurer will be responsible for transferring a batch payment to Wellington Kyudo Club.

How to pay (international participants):
Please pay via your national Kyudo organisation's bank account and inform the secretary.
Your national Kyudo organisation's secretary/treasurer will be responsible for transferring a batch payment to Wellington Kyudo Club.
