Auckland Kyudo Seminar October 2024

This seminar is for NZKF and IKYF registered practitioners only.

Date:
Saturday 26th — Sunday 27th October 2024

Location:
The Y Lagoon Stadium, 44 Domain Road, Panmure, Auckland, New Zealand

Group dinner on Saturday night:
TBC - information will be emailed to participants, and posted on this page, when available.

Fees and payment

Payments are non-refundable, since we would have spent the money on venue/accommodation deposits.

Seminar fee:
130 NZD (includes two packed lunches)

How to pay (NZKF members):
Please pay via your local club bank account and inform your club's committee.
Your club's secretary and treasurer will be responsible for transferring a batch payment to Auckland Kyudo Club.

How to pay (international participants):
Please pay via your national Kyudo organisation's bank account and inform the secretary.
Your national Kyudo organisation's secretary/treasurer will be responsible for transferring a batch payment to Auckland Kyudo Club.

Registration and payment deadline:
29th September 2024. No extension
