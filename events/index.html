Events

Seminars are for registered NZ Kyudo Federation members and overseas practitioners only.

Auckland Kyudo Seminar October 2024

Date: Saturday 26th — Sunday 27th October 2024

Location: The Y Lagoon Stadium, 44 Domain Road, Panmure, Auckland, New Zealand

Registration and payment deadline: 29th September 2024 - No extension

Fees and payment

Payments are non-refundable, since we would have spent the money on venue/accommodation deposits.

Seminar fee (includes two packed lunches): 130 NZD

How to pay (NZKF members):
Please pay via your local club bank account and inform your club's committee.
Your club's secretary and treasurer will be responsible for transferring a batch payment to Auckland Kyudo Club.

How to pay (international participants):
Please pay via your national federation bank account and inform the secretary.
The national secretary/treasurer will be responsible for transferring a batch payment to Auckland Kyudo Club.
